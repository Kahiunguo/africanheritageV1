Our Story

Rooted in African tradition, crafted for modern hair care

The African Heritage Legacy

For centuries, African communities have relied on the power of natural botanicals to nurture their hair. From the fertile lands of West Africa to the sun-drenched plains of East Africa, generations have passed down knowledge of potent herbs, oils, and roots that promote hair health and growth.

Our founder, Amina Nkosi, grew up watching her grandmother prepare special hair oils using recipes perfected over decades. Inspired by this heritage, she set out to create a product that honors these traditions while meeting the needs of contemporary hair care.

At African Heritage Hair Oils, we meticulously source our ingredients from ethical suppliers across the African continent, ensuring each bottle captures the essence of our cultural legacy.

How To Use

1

Apply to Scalp

Part hair and apply oil directly to scalp using the dropper

2

Massage Gently

Massage in circular motions for 3-5 minutes to stimulate follicles

3

Distribute Through Hair

Apply remaining oil through the length of your hair

4

Leave In

For best results, leave in overnight or for at least 30 minutes

Recommended: Use 2–3 times per week for optimal results
